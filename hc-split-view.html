Create a Project
How to create a project
Follow these steps in your Todoist account on the right
1
Open the sidebar

In the left sidebar of Todoist, scroll down to the My Projects section.

2
Click the + button

Hover over My Projects and click the + icon that appears to the right of it.

You can also use the keyboard shortcut G then P to go straight to Projects.
3
Name your project

Type a name for your project in the field that appears (e.g. "Work", "Personal", "Shopping list").

4
Choose a color (optional)

Click the color swatch to pick a color that helps you visually identify this project.

5
Add to Favorites (optional)

Toggle Add to Favorites to pin the project to the top of your sidebar for quick access.

6
Click Add

Hit Add to save the project. It will now appear in your sidebar under My Projects.

You can add tasks to your new project by clicking into it and pressing + or hitting Q for Quick Add.
Your project is ready — start adding tasks!
